Game Rules

Literature is a card game played with 2 teams. The goal is to collect complete sets of cards.

The game uses a standard 52-card deck with all 8s removed. Cards are grouped into 8 sets: Low Hearts, High Hearts, Low Diamonds, High Diamonds, etc.
